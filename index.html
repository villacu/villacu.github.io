JusticIA para los desaparecidos.

This Hackathon was organized by RIIA and was aimed at helping clarify political disappearances in Mexico, so the task was to develop solutions to automatically read hundreds of (low quality) scanned military documents and perform object detection in order to detect signatures, fingerprints, etc. Our team proposed a UNet to perform document binarization in low quality document photos and using yolov5 along with data augmentation to detect the objects in the picture. Our submission obtained an honorific mention.
