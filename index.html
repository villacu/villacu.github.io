Aggresive Language Detection using BERT-based models


For this project, I am currently working with spanish BERT (Bidirectitonal Enconder Representation Transformers) models, that are trained on the taks of classifying aggressive language and hate speech in social media.
The datasets that currently exist for these tasks are relatively small, this makes hard to reliably train complex models obtaining highly different results depending on the weight initialization. Our research is centered around training more stable models by using different techniques.

The obtained results achieve state of the art results in different spanish-datasets, and we are currently in progress of writing a research paper.

This work is being carried out in my position as a RA at the Mathematics Research Center under supervision of Dr. Pastor Lopez. The code will be available on GitHub as soon as it is possible.
